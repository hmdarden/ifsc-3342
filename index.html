  • Final Site Planning:
      Questionnaire
      1. Who is the target audience?
        • Avid gamers and movie/television buffs
        • This could appeal to a wide age range
      2. How can I tailor the web site to reach that audience?
        • Have interesting images and graphics that appeal to the younger and more tech savvy audience
        • Make the page easy to navigate for older and less tech savvy audiences
      3. What are the goals for the site?
        • Be informative
        • Keep readers and viewers engaged
        • Have a section or page dedicated to the video game
        • Have a section or page dedicated to the tv show
        • Give readers and viewers options to purchase collectables and other items or provide them with links on where they can purchase
      4. How will I gather the information?
        • A large part of the information I gather will be from gaming sites and review sites
        • Another part will come from personal experience with the game and tv show
      5. What are my sources for multimedia content?
        • Any content that I can find online
        • To be determined if I will add personally taken multimedia
      6. What is my budget?
        • Not high, but willing to spend when needed
      7. How long do I have to complete the project?
        • End of the semester
      8. Who is on my project team?
        • Solo project
      9. How often should the site be updated?
        • Whenever a new season, game, or collectables release
      10. Who is responsible for updating the site?
        • Myself
